Hey, I'm Parth Batra.

ML Engineer by day, music critic by night.

I grew up in Gurgaon—a small town near Delhi-NCR—and have been navigating the data science landscape for about four years now.

Right now, I'm a Senior ML Engineer at Procol (mostly, procol.ai), wrangling data and building AI solutions. Before this, my journey included summer internship at Maruti Suzuki , AI engineering at Genpact and software engineering at Brightchamps and Munch.

I call myself a Full Stack ML Engineer because I enjoy the entire lifecycle:

  • Building data pipelines that actually work
  • Training ML models that don't just look pretty on slides
  • Creating LLM agents that might just flirt with the Turing test
  • Crafting SQL queries epic enough for their own GitHub repo
  • Developing microservices with CI/CD pipelines that deploy without breaking
  • Designing dashboards that turn business metrics into "aha" insights

I launched this blog to share my unfiltered thoughts—whether it's breaking down cutting-edge tech or defending controversial hot takes (like why R secretly crushes Python for clean code). It's raw, sometimes messy, but always authentic—like a journal that accidentally went public.

I graduated from BITS Pilani with a dual-degree flex: B.E. (Hons.) in Mechanical Engineering and M.S. (Hons.) in Mathematics. And yeah, I've logged over 100k minutes on Spotify Rewind in the last three years— arguably my bigger life achievement.

When I'm not wrangling data or debugging code, I'm deep in POP-culture rabbit holes, grabbing popcorn for Twitter meltdowns, or discovering artists before they hit your Discover Weekly. Check out my articles if that sounds like your vibe.
